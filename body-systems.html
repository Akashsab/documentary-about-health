Body Systems

Circulatory System

The circulatory system consists of the heart, blood vessels, and blood. It is responsible for transporting nutrients, oxygen, and hormones throughout the body.

Respiratory System

The respiratory system includes the lungs and airways. It facilitates the exchange of oxygen and carbon dioxide between the air and the bloodstream.
